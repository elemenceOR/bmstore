Our Story

Since 1986, Badulla Motor Stores has been the trusted name for quality vehicle spare parts in the Badulla region.

With over 38 years of experience, we've built our reputation on delivering genuine, high-quality parts for all major vehicle brands. Our commitment to authenticity, competitive pricing, and expert customer service has made us the go-to destination for both individual vehicle owners and professional mechanics.

We maintain strong relationships with authorized manufacturers and suppliers, ensuring that every part we sell meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.
